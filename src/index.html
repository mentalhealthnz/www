Welcome to our little place on the internet where we share news, stories, events and things surrounding mental health.

The IT industry can be a lonely place sometimes. Mental health issues, a lack of confidence or lack of support can make work more stressful, isolating and difficult than it needs to be. You may feel like you’re alone.

Well you’re not. Many in the industry suffer the same pervasive thoughts. Regardless of whether you’re a designer, developer, project manager or student, navigating the world of IT can be difficult.

We want to change this!
