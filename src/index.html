  • Kindness Matters:
    The power of mindfulness and self-compassion

    Speaker: Anna Friis
    Self-compassion is emerging as a balm for many modern day ailments, arising out of scientific studies into the health benefits associated with mindfulness. Not only does self-compassion increase emotional resilience but also protects against the negative effects of stress on wellbeing and physical health. Anna will talk about how mindfulness and self-compassion are skills that can be learned by anyone, bringing a greater sense of ease and happiness, and enabling one to ”breath underwater” when life feels overwhelming.
